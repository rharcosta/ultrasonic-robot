## 📟 LIST OF MATERIALS
* Male-female jumper cables;
* Male-to-male jumper cables;
* 1 mini protoboard board;
* 2 engines;
* 2 wheels;
* 1 support chassis;
* 1 ultrasonic sensor;
* 1 H bridge;
* 1 Arduino;
* 1 battery.
